Logo Usage

White version — use on all dark slides (blue-950, blue-900, blue-800 backgrounds). Position: bottom-right, height 40–48px, 110px from right edge, 34px from bottom.
Color version — use on light slides (white, neutral-50). Same position rules apply.
EU / ERC funding lockup — required on the closing slide only. Height 52–64px, bottom-right, matching logo margin.

05

Decorative Elements & Recurring Devices

Eyebrow / section label

Gold 2px rule + IBM Plex Mono uppercase, 24px (dark: Blue 300 / light: Blue 600). Placed above every slide title.

Title underline rule

60px wide, 3px tall, Gold 400 (#E2AD07), border-radius 2px. Placed immediately below every slide title.

Bullet style

Gold 9–10px filled circle, 30px indent. Font: IBM Plex Sans 34px (light slides), Blue 100 on dark. Gap: 26–28px between items.

Pull quote

"Quote text in IBM Plex Sans italic, 30px on slides."

4px Gold 400 left border, 36–40px padding-left. Used for speaker lines and attribution. On dark slides: border stays gold, text is Blue 200.
